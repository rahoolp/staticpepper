A La Carte Services

Each our engagements begin with a comprehensive discussion with the product owner, possible market opportunties, define who the customer is ad how to create appeal to the target customer segament. We understand your organization's or your values, priorities, and preferences, as well as how we fit together.

  • Brand Creation

    Branding

    From identifying a name and corresponding logo, to securing web domain names, searching for trade marks, applying for trade marks to securing social media handles we can help and connect you with our network of cost effective service providers


    Packaging for best customer experinnce

    From outer packaging to innner, labeling to package material type to color scheme we guide you or evaluate what you already have

  • Photography, Listing

    Product Photography and Videography

    We best present your products with best suited hero shots and lifestyle pictures to speak to the day to day use of your product


    Listing management

    Listing with appropriate content that suits various shopping platforms, search engines. Details that tell a compelling a story of your product and call for action to drive conversion.

  • Digital Marketing

    Target Customer Identification

    For the niche product you have we identify who is your target customer and help you understand what will resonate well and how to message and connect with the customer


    Identify markets and channels

    What geographic market to take the product to and what channels to use based on what is prevelent in the market and where customer shops. This includes social media to engage with the customer and taking efforts to build Facebook communities.


    Content Creation

    Create content that will appeal to customers and messages that will resonate. From pictures to blogs of stories around the product and behind the scenes of a making product to artists life that depends on the work they do we help you craft the content.


    Media Advertising

    We setup online campaigns on Facebook, Google, Amazon, Pinterest and so on for product and content discovery and engagement with the brand and ultimately achieve conversion goals.

  • Logistics

    Import Export, Customs Clearance, Freight Forwarding

    Your product needs to be exported from your country to the market where it will sell. We handle the export and customs formalities through our own compeny and through our partners. This includes the import side of logistics as well in terms of paying customs duty, cleanring the goods to forwarding them to the warehouse.


    Ground, air and ocean shipments

    We provide logistics services that can pick-up goods from where you are or you can ship them to our in-country agent's facility or you can even directly export to our overseas warehouse. We ship goods via air in courier and commercial mode or ocean. From parcels to few cartons to pallets we can ship it all.


    Compliance

    Pepperment can export goods on its own from India and file documementaion for customs clearance and handle customs duty payments in importing counties.

  • Warehousing

    In-market inventory

    Customer expect faster turn around and fast shipment, in order to provide the best experience we bring the inventory to the market where customer is. This sometimes involves exporting goods from your country and importing them in the markets we serve (US, Canada, UK)


    Customer order fulfillemt

    We fulfill orders to customers offering different shipping tiers


    Returns and Exchanges

    Returns are handled and goods are inspected for possibly resaling them or sell as refurbsihed items or dispose them.
